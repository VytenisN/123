American football team Kaunas "Dukes"



I am one of the founders and manager of American football team Kaunas "Dukes". This project started in 2016 and got serious during 2017 when we started to train with the full equipment.

I have organised first american football game in Kaunas where Dukes played an exibition game in front of a crowd of ~200 people.

We continue to put effort in order to make the game more popular and recruit new players to try out the sport. The main goal is to build a community with passion and connection which was made on the field but expands off the field as well.
